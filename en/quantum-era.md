# Quantum Computing, Cybersecurity, and Strategic Superiority

Quantum computers have the theoretical power to break today's most complex encryption algorithms in seconds. This technological leap will reshape not only the world of informatics but also **international power balances**.

## Cybersecurity Zero Point

With the emergence of quantum computers, the reliability of traditional encryption methods like RSA and ECC will drop to zero. This risks leaving all data flows critical to military and national security vulnerable.
